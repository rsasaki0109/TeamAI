# Code of Conduct

TeamAI Runtime contributors are expected to keep technical discussion respectful,
focused, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being dismissive.
- Critique code, designs, and tradeoffs instead of people.
- Assume good intent, but correct harmful or unsafe behavior clearly.
- Keep security-sensitive reports private until maintainers can respond.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Deliberate disruption of project discussions
- Publishing private security reports without coordination
- Discriminatory language or behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation
when behavior harms the project or its contributors. Security reports are
handled under `SECURITY.md`.
